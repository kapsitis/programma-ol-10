**1.** Pārveido vienādojumu formā $Ax + By + C = 0$, kur $A$, $B$ un $C$
ir veseli skaitļi!

  -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  1\.   $$y = 5x - 8$$                       1\.   $$y = 2x + 5$$
  ----- ------------------------------------ ----- ------------------------------------
  2\.   $$y = \frac{2}{7}x + \frac{3}{7}$$   2\.   $$y = \frac{3}{8}x - \frac{5}{8}$$

  3\.   $$y = 0,3x + 4$$                     3\.   $$y = 0,4x - 3$$

  4\.   $$y = - \frac{3}{11}x + 2$$          4\.   $$y = - \frac{5}{13}x + 3$$
  -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**2.** Pārveido vienādojumu formā $y = kx + b$!

  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
  1\.   $$2x + 3y = 7$$                 1\.   $$3x + 2y = 5$$
  ----- ------------------------------- ----- -------------------------------
  2\.   $$3x - 5y = 1$$                 2\.   $$4x - 2y = 7$$

  3\.   $$- 4x - 6y = 3$$               3\.   $$- 2x - 3y = 5$$
  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
