# FVD 1 – izteiksmes sadalīšana reizinātājos

Sadali izteiksmi reizinātājos!
|

**(1)** (1 p.) $y^3-125x^3=$ 

$\color{blue}{y^3-(5x)^3=(y-5x)(y^2+5xy+25x^2)}$

**(2)** (1 p.) $8a^3+27b^3=$ 

$\color{blue}{(2a)^3+(3b)^3=(2a+3b)(4a^2-6ab+9b^2)}$

**(3)** (2 p.) $2m+6-mz-3z=$ 

$\color{blue}{2(m+3)-z(m+3)=(m+3)(2-z)}$

**(4)** (2 p.) $3(2x-y)+m(y-2x)=$ 

$\color{blue}{3(2x-y)-m(2x-y)=(2x-y)(3-m)}$

**(5)** (2 p.) $x^2+4x-12=$ 

$\color{blue}{(x+6)(x-2)}$

$\color{blue}{\text{pēc Vjeta t.}}\quad \color{blue}{\begin{array}{l} x_1\cdot x_2=-12\\ x_1+x_2=-4\end{array}$

$x_1=-6;\ x_2=2}$

**(6)** (2 p.) $2x^2-x-3=$ 

$\color{blue}{2(x-1{,}5)(x+1)=(2x-3)(x+1)}$

$\color{blue}{D=(-1)^2-4\cdot2\cdot(-3)=1+24=25}$

$\color{blue}{x_1=\frac{1+5}{2\cdot2}=\frac{6}{4}=1{,}5$

$x_2=\frac{1-5}{4}=\frac{-4}{4}=-1}$

**(7)** (3 p.) $c^2+d^2+2cd-9=$ 

$\color{blue}{(c+d)^2-3^2=(c+d-3)(c+d+3)}$
