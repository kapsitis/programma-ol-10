# 1. temats. Sadalīšana reizinātājos un vienādojumu atrisināšanas metodes

# 1. variants

*Vārds, uzvārds, klase*

## 1. (___/8 punkti) Sadali reizinātājos!

### 1.1. (1 p.)

$$5a^3b^2-15ab^3 = \color{blue}{5ab^2(a-3b)}$$

### 1.2. (2 p.)

$$2y(3x-5)-9x+15 = \color{blue}{2y(3x-5)-3(3x-5) = (3x-5)(2y-3)}$$

### 1.3. (2 p.)

$$1-64a^3 = \color{blue}{1^3-(4a)^3 = (1-4a)(1+4a+16a^2)}$$

### 1.4. (3 p.)

$$3x^2-7x+2 = \color{blue}{3(x-2)\left(x-\dfrac{1}{3}\right) = (x-2)(3x-1)}$$

$$\color{blue}{D=(-7)^2-4\cdot 3\cdot 2 = 49-24=25}$$

$$\color{blue}{x_1=\dfrac{7+5}{2\cdot 3}=\dfrac{12}{6}=2 \; ; \quad x_2=\dfrac{7-5}{6}=\dfrac{2}{6}=\dfrac{1}{3}}$$

## 2. (___/9 punkti) Atrisini vienādojumus!

### 2.1. (2 p.)

$$x(x-1)(x+3)=0$$

$$\color{blue}{x=0 \; \text{vai} \; x-1=0 \; \text{vai} \; x+3=0}$$

$$\color{blue}{x_1=0} \qquad \color{blue}{x_2=1} \qquad \color{blue}{x_3=-3}$$

### 2.2. (3 p.)

$$x^3-3x^2-10x=0$$

$$\color{blue}{x(x^2-3x-10)=0}$$

$$\color{blue}{x_1=0 \; \text{vai} \; x^2-3x-10=0}$$

$$\color{blue}{\text{pēc atbita t. } \begin{cases} x_2\cdot x_3=-10 \\ x_2+x_3=3 \end{cases}}$$

$$\color{blue}{x_2=5 \; ; \quad x_3=-2}$$

### 2.3. (4 p.)

$$x^4-5x^2+4=0$$

$$\color{blue}{x^2=t}$$

$$\color{blue}{t^2-5t+4=0}$$

$$\color{blue}{\text{pēc atbita t. } \begin{cases} t_1\cdot t_2=4 \\ t_1+t_2=5 \end{cases}}$$

$$\color{blue}{t_1=1 \; ; \quad t_2=4}$$

$$\color{blue}{x^2=1 \qquad\qquad x^2=4}$$

$$\color{blue}{x_{1,2}=\pm 1 \qquad\qquad x_{3,4}=\pm 2}$$



## 3. (___/4 punkti) Atrisini doto vienādojumu $x^4-x=0$ grafiski!

$$\color{blue}{x^4-x=0}$$
$$\color{blue}{x^4=x}$$
$$\color{blue}{y=x^4 \; ; \quad y=x}$$

$$\color{blue}{
\begin{array}{c|c|c|c|c|c}
x & -2 & -1 & 0 & 1 & 2 \\ \hline
y & 16 & 1 & 0 & 1 & 16
\end{array}
}$$

$$\color{blue}{
\begin{array}{c|c|c|c}
x & -2 & 0 & 2 \\ \hline
y & -2 & 0 & 2
\end{array}
}$$

![](OL_1_npd_ATR.A.png)

$$\color{blue}{\text{Atbilde: } x_1=0 \; ; \quad x_2=1}$$

## 4. (___/3 punkti) Atrisini vienādojumu!

$$(x^2+2x)^2-14x^2-28x-15=0$$

$$\color{blue}{(x^2+2x)^2-14(x^2+2x)-15=0}$$

$$\color{blue}{x^2+2x=t}$$

$$\color{blue}{t^2-14t-15=0}$$

$$\color{blue}{\text{pēc atbita t. } \begin{cases} t_1\cdot t_2=-15 \\ t_1+t_2=14 \end{cases}}$$

$$\color{blue}{t_1=15 \; ; \quad t_2=-1}$$

$$\color{blue}{x^2+2x=15 \qquad\qquad x^2+2x=-1}$$
$$\color{blue}{x^2+2x-15=0 \qquad\qquad x^2+2x+1=0}$$
$$\color{blue}{x_1=-5;\; x_2=3 \qquad\qquad x_{3,4}=-1}$$

## 5. (___/2 punkti) Sadali izteiksmi $m^8-n^8$ vismaz trijos reizinātājos!

$$\color{blue}{m^8-n^8 = (m^4-n^4)(m^4+n^4) = (m^2-n^2)(m^2+n^2)(m^4+n^4) =}$$
$$\color{blue}{= (m-n)(m+n)(m^2+n^2)(m^4+n^4)}$$

## 1. (___/8 punkti) Sadali reizinātājos!

### 1.1. (1 p.)

$$18xy^4-6x^2y^2 = \color{blue}{6xy^2(3y^2-x)}$$

### 1.2. (2 p.)

$$3a(2b-1)-4b+2 = \color{blue}{3a(2b-1)-2(2b-1) = (2b-1)(3a-2)}$$

### 1.3. (2 p.)

$$8a^3-1 = \color{blue}{(2a)^3-1^3 = (2a-1)(4a^2+2a+1)}$$

### 1.4. (3 p.)

$$4x^2+x-3 = \color{blue}{4\left(x-\dfrac{3}{4}\right)(x+1) = (4x-3)(x+1)}$$

$$\color{blue}{D=1^2-4\cdot 4\cdot(-3) = 1+48=49}$$

$$\color{blue}{x_1=\dfrac{-1+7}{2\cdot 4}=\dfrac{6}{8}=\dfrac{3}{4} \; ; \quad x_2=\dfrac{-1-7}{8}=\dfrac{-8}{8}=-1}$$

## 2. (___/9 punkti) Atrisini vienādojumus!

### 2.1. (2 p.)

$$x(x+2)(x-3)=0$$

$$\color{blue}{x=0 \; \text{vai} \; x+2=0 \; \text{vai} \; x-3=0}$$

$$\color{blue}{x_1=0} \qquad \color{blue}{x_2=-2} \qquad \color{blue}{x_3=3}$$

### 2.2. (3 p.)

$$x^3+3x^2-10x=0$$

$$\color{blue}{x(x^2+3x-10)=0}$$

$$\color{blue}{x_1=0 \; \text{vai} \; x^2+3x-10=0}$$

$$\color{blue}{\text{pēc atbita t. } \begin{cases} x_2\cdot x_3=-10 \\ x_2+x_3=-3 \end{cases}}$$

$$\color{blue}{x_2=-5 \; ; \quad x_3=2}$$

### 2.3. (4 p.)

$$x^4-17x^2+16=0$$

$$\color{blue}{x^2=t}$$

$$\color{blue}{t^2-17t+16=0}$$

$$\color{blue}{\text{pēc atbita t. } \begin{cases} t_1\cdot t_2=16 \\ t_1+t_2=17 \end{cases}}$$

$$\color{blue}{t_1=1 \; ; \quad t_2=16}$$

$$\color{blue}{x^2=1 \qquad\qquad x^2=16}$$

$$\color{blue}{x_{1,2}=\pm 1 \qquad\qquad x_{3,4}=\pm 4}$$

## 4. (___/3 punkti) Atrisini vienādojumu!

$$(x^2-2x)^2-14x^2+28x-15=0$$

## 5. (___/2 punkti) Sadali izteiksmi $a^8-b^8$ vismaz trijos reizinātājos!

$$\color{blue}{= (a-b)(a+b)(a^2+b^2)(a^4+b^4)}$$
