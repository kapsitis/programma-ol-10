# FVD 2 – vienādojumu risināšana ar substitūcijas metodi

## 1. Atrisini vienādojumu!

### 1.1. (4 p.)

$$x^4 - 10x^2 + 9 = 0$$

$$\color{blue}{\text{Apz. } x^2=t}$$

$$\color{blue}{t^2-10t+9=0}$$

$$\color{blue}{t=1 \qquad t=9}$$

$$\color{blue}{x^2=1 \qquad x^2=9}$$

$$\color{blue}{x=\pm 1 \qquad x=\pm 3}$$

### 1.2. (4 p.)

$$(x^2+2)^2-5(x^2+2)+6=0$$

$$\color{blue}{\text{Apz. } x^2+2=t}$$

$$\color{blue}{t^2-5t+6=0}$$

$$\color{blue}{t=2 \qquad t=3}$$

$$\color{blue}{x^2+2=2 \qquad x^2+2=3}$$

$$\color{blue}{x^2=0 \qquad x^2=1}$$

$$\color{blue}{x=0 \qquad x=\pm 1}$$

## 3. (4 p.) Ievies jaunu mainīgo un pārveido iegūto vienādojumu ar jauno mainīgo pamatformā! Uzraksti divus dažādus veidus!

$$(x^2+3x)(x^2+3x-2)=8$$

### 1. veids

$$\color{blue}{\text{Apz. } x^2+3x=t}$$

$$\color{blue}{t(t-2)=8}$$

$$\boxed{\color{blue}{t^2-2t-8=0}}$$

$$\color{blue}{t=4 \qquad t=-2}$$

$$
\color{blue}{
\left.\begin{array}{l}
x^2+3x=4\\
x^2+3x-4=0\\
x=-4,\ x=1
\end{array}\quad
\begin{array}{l}
x^2+3x=-2\\
x^2+3x+2=0\\
x=-1,\ x=-2
\end{array}\right\}\ \text{Nav obligāts}
}
$$

### 2. veids

$$(x^2+3x)(x^2+3x-2)=8$$

$$\color{blue}{\text{Apz. } x^2+3x-2=t}$$

$$\color{blue}{x^2+3x=t+2}$$

$$\color{blue}{(t+2)t=8}$$

$$\boxed{\color{blue}{t^2+2t-8=0}}$$

$$\color{blue}{t=-4 \qquad t=2}$$

$$
\color{blue}{
\left.\begin{array}{l}
x^2+3x-2=-4\\
x^2+3x+2=0\\
x=-1,\ x=-2
\end{array}\quad
\begin{array}{l}
x^2+3x-2=2\\
x^2+3x-4=0\\
x=-4,\ x=1
\end{array}\right\}\ \text{Nav obligāts}
}
$$
